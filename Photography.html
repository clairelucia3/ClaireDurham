Perspective Shapes Life.

As part of my introductory photography class, I completed a final project exploring one of the themes of photography.

Project Brainstorm
When brainstorming for this project, I focused on discovering circles in everyday objects from different angles, emphasizing how perspective can reveal hidden patterns in the world around us. I wanted to incorporate a play on words—"Perspective Shapes Life"—to highlight how shifting viewpoints can transform the way we see both art and life.
In the process,
I used Photoshop and masks, in attempt to make the subject dark and emphasize the circular object with light. This proved to be difficult and some images did not come out as I wished as seen in the two images below where the exposure was too high or I the camera was not focused properly.
Final Project
This class introduced me to cameras, Photoshop, Lightroom, and other essential editing tools. This experience allowed me to develop a strong foundation in digital design and photo manipulation, helping me refine my creative skills.
