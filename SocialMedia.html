Pioneer Painters

I was asked to develope multiple brand logos to be used across their business website and printed shirts.

Step One: Sketch and Brainstorm
I was asked to make a logo that was clean and easily remembered. They were drawn to the color light purple and wanted to include that in the branding.
Step Two: Work and Play
Next I played around with colors and vectors. I think that it is important to push boundaries as well so I played along with a logo that meshed together a paint brush and a pioneer wagon.
Step Three: Put it all together!
After finalizing the design, I composed it all into a branding file where I showcased each logo that I had made. I also made sure to record the colors used so that it will come in use for the future website.
